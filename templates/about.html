Pre-release Prediction of Crowd Opinion on Movies by Label Distribution Learning

We implemented a LDL algorithm to predict the crowd opinion on movies. Basing on five-year movie data crawled from IMDB website, we manage to predict ratings of recent coming movies with the algorithm, comparing the prediction with real rating result on this website. Specifically, we evaluate movies before they are released. In order to prove that we indeed do the prediction in advance, blockchain technology is applied to generate timestamps for each prediction. Its details and mechanism can be found here.

The whole project was completed under Prof. Geng's instruction in SEU PALM lab.
